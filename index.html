About us

Our Mission

To ensure active participation of Young Quantity Surveyors at National/Chapter programmes and stimulate effective representation at international events. To develop and enhance the leadership skills of young Quantity Surveyors. To provide a platform for intellectual discussion, brainstorming sessions, capacity building with the aim of promoting Quantity Surveying profession and continuous improvement of professional practices and standards. To undertake research, study and collate information regarding the latest development and technological advancement in the field of Quantity Surveying and refer research outcomes to the Nigerian Institute of Quantity Surveyors

Our Vision

To be a premier organization of young professionals in Nigeria, contributing to the growth of natural and built environment, and by extension the overall economy.
